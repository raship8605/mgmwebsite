Welcome to MGM College of CS & IT

A premier institution for computer science and information technology education in Maharashtra

Established in 2000, MGM College of Computer Science & IT has been at the forefront of providing quality education in the field of computer science and information technology. Our institution is committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of the digital age.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in computer science and IT that transforms students into competent professionals with strong ethical values, capable of contributing to society and the nation.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in computer science and IT education, fostering innovation, research, and entrepreneurship among students.
